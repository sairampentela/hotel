hotel faculites

free wifi

Stay connected effortlessly during your stay with our complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi available throughout the hotel. Whether you're catching up on work, streaming your favorite shows, or staying in touch with loved ones, enjoy seamless internet access in all rooms and public areas

swimming pool

Dive into relaxation at our sparkling clean, well-maintained swimming pool, perfect for a refreshing break. Whether you want a morning swim, an afternoon lounge by the poolside, or a relaxing evening dip, our pool is the ideal place to unwind. Separate kids' pool available for family fun!

gym

Stay fit while you travel in our state-of-the-art gym, open daily for all guests. Featuring modern cardio machines, strength training equipment, and a clean, spacious workout area, our fitness center helps you maintain your routine with ease. Whether you're into light workouts or intense sessions, we’ve got you covered.

restaurant

Savor a delightful dining experience at our multi-cuisine in-house restaurant, offering a wide range of Indian, Continental, and Chinese dishes. Whether it’s a hearty breakfast, a relaxing lunch, or a romantic dinner, our expert chefs ensure every meal is a treat. Room service and customizable meal options available for your comfort.

Free parking

Enjoy the convenience of complimentary on-site parking for all our guests. Whether you're driving in for a business trip or a family vacation, rest assured knowing your vehicle is safe with us — 24/7 secure and spacious parking available at no extra charge

Lift

Our hotel is equipped with a modern and spacious lift, providing easy access to all floors. Whether you're carrying luggage or prefer a more convenient way to move around, our lift ensures a comfortable and hassle-free experience for all guests, including seniors and differently-abled individuals.

Laundry Service

We offer quick, reliable, and professional laundry service to keep you looking your best during your stay. From washing and ironing to same-day delivery, our staff ensures your clothes are fresh and neatly pressed. Simply request through reception or room service for convenient pickup and drop-off

Air Conditioning

Enjoy a cool and comfortable stay with our efficient air-conditioned rooms. Each room is equipped with modern climate control to maintain your preferred temperature, ensuring a pleasant environment no matter the weather outside.

Safe Locker

For your peace of mind, each room is equipped with a secure electronic safe locker to store your valuables, important documents, and personal belongings. Easy to use and fully secure, our lockers ensure your items are safe and protected throughout your stay

Airport Pickup & Drop

Make your journey hassle-free with our convenient airport transfer service. We offer timely pickup and drop to and from the airport, ensuring you travel comfortably and on schedule. Our friendly drivers and well-maintained vehicles are ready to welcome you, whether you’re arriving or departing.

Garden / Lawn Area

Relax and unwind in our beautifully maintained garden and lawn area, a peaceful oasis amidst the city buzz. Perfect for morning walks, reading, or enjoying a cup of tea, our green spaces provide a refreshing environment for guests of all ages.
